誰是初音未來?

介紹

初音未來是一位全球性的流行偶像，但她實際上是 Vocaloid——一種由 Crypton Future Media 開發的歌聲合成軟體。 她的名字翻譯成「未來的第一個聲音」。 她的官方形象是一位留著標誌性長蔥色雙馬尾的 16 歲少女。初音未來的聲音是基於日本聲優藤田咲的聲音樣本。

What is Hatsune Miku?

introduction

Hatsune Miku is a global pop sensation, but she is actually a Vocaloid a singing voice synthesizer software developed by Crypton Future Media. Her name translates to "The first sound of the future." She is officially depicted as a 16-year-old girl with iconic long, turquoise twintails. Miku's voice is based on vocal samples from the Japanese voice actress Saki Fujita.

My Name

初音未來是日本Crypton Future Media公司基於Yamaha VOCALOID語音合成技術所開發的虛擬女性歌手，以其綠色雙馬尾和獨特服裝的動漫形象聞名。 她於2007年8月31日推出，由日本配音演員藤田咲的聲音為原型，擅長演唱流行歌曲。 初音未來不僅是一個電子樂器軟體，她還是一個具有人氣的虛擬角色，廣泛出現在音樂、遊戲等領域。
Hatsune Miku is virtual female singer developed by the Japanese company Crypton Future Media, based on Yamaha's VOCALOID voice synthesis technology. She is famous for her anime-style appearance with green twintails and a unique costume. She was launched on August 31, 2007, and her voice is modeled after the Japanese voice actress Saki Fujita. She excels at singing pop songs. Hatsune Miku is not just electronic instrument software; she is also a popular virtual character who widely appears in music, games, and other fields.
